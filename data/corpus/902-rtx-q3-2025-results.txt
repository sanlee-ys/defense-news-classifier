RTX Reports Q3 2025 Results

ARLINGTON, Va., October 21, 2025 – RTX (NYSE: RTX) reports third quarter 2025 results. RTX delivered 12% sales growth with strong operational performance, raising its 2025 outlook for adjusted sales and adjusted EPS and confirming free cash flow.

Third quarter 2025 sales were $22.5 billion, up 12 percent versus the prior year, and up 13 percent organically excluding divestitures. GAAP EPS was $1.41 and adjusted EPS was $1.70, up 17 percent versus the prior year. Operating cash flow was $4.6 billion and free cash flow was $4.0 billion. Company backlog stood at $251 billion, including $148 billion of commercial and $103 billion of defense.

"Strong execution in the third quarter enabled us to deliver double-digit organic sales growth across all three segments and our sixth consecutive quarter of year-over-year adjusted segment margin expansion," said RTX Chairman and CEO Chris Calio. "We also received $37 billion of new awards in the quarter, reflecting robust global demand for our products and supporting long-term growth for RTX."
