L3Harris Technologies Reports Strong First Quarter 2026 Results

MELBOURNE, Fla., April 30, 2026 — L3Harris Technologies (NYSE: LHX) reports first quarter 2026 results. Orders of $7.8 billion produced a book-to-bill of 1.4x, increasing backlog to a new record $40.7 billion. Revenue of $5.7 billion was up 12%, and 15% organically. Operating margin of 11.4% was up 120 basis points, and GAAP diluted EPS of $2.72 was up 33%.

"We continue to execute against our Trusted Disruptor strategy with both urgency and discipline, leveraging speed and scale to meet the evolving needs of our customers. Our results reflect the strength of our portfolio and its alignment with the nation's most critical defense missions," said Christopher Kubasik, Chairman and CEO. "Across the portfolio, we quickly adapt to mission needs and scale our operations to meet increasing demand, accelerating growth across our enterprise."

Revenue grew across all three segments — Space & Mission Systems, Communication & Spectrum Dominance, and Missile Solutions — reflecting new program ramps and increased international volume. The company updated its 2026 EPS guidance to $11.40 - $11.60.
