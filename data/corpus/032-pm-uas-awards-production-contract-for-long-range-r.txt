PM UAS Awards Production Contract for Long Range Reconnaissance Systems to AV

REDSTONE ARSENAL, AL – March 20, 2026 – The Capability Program Executive (CPE), Aviation's Uncrewed Aircraft Systems Project Office (UAS PO) awarded a production contract to AV for Long Range Reconnaissance (LRR) systems. These AV P550 were procured through the Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS) Marketplace Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA).
LRR UAS systems provide maneuver Battalion Commanders with enhanced organic reconnaissance, surveillance, and target acquisition capabilities, increasing situational awareness and standoff from potential threats on the modern battlefield.
"This award is a testament to the Army's commitment to rapid and agile acquisition," said COL Danielle Medaglia, Project Manager for Uncrewed Aircraft Systems. "The UAS Marketplace reduces traditional, lengthy procurement timelines to a few days. This allows us to get the most advanced capabilities into the hands of our Soldiers at the speed of relevance, ensuring they are equipped to meet current and future challenges."
This procurement marks a significant step in the modernization of the Army's tactical UAS portfolio, providing a crucial advantage for ground forces in complex and contested environments.
About PM UAS:
PM UAS, under the Capabilities Program Executive - Aviation, is responsible for the development, acquisition, fielding, and life cycle management of the Army's fleet of uncrewed aircraft systems. By working with industry partners, PO UAS ensures that Soldiers are equipped with the most advanced and reliable UAS technology.
About AV:
AV is a global leader in intelligent, multi-domain robotic systems and serves defense, government, and commercial customers. AV's portfolio of uncrewed systems and related services delivers actionable intelligence, enhances situational awareness, and provides critical capabilities for a wide range of missions.
